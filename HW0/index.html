Shoot Man

  • Originality: The game is a platformer that focuses on the player being able to bounce a bullet at an enemy.
  • Technical Merit: Bullets are able to bounce off of the world boundaries and platforms. With this, the player can try more interesting shots.
  • Prototype Postmortem: Having to make a game via code is time consuming, and having to look up every something.somethingElse property makes it worse. Debugging with the dev console in Chrome really made things easier.
  • Prototype Assets: I made all of my assets from scratch. The images were made in photoshop and the sound effectes were made in bfxr.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: While I didn't borrow code/mechanics from a particular game or Phaser Example, I took inspiration from the puzzle platformer genre.
